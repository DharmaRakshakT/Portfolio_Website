Forecasting Pound Value using various Macro economic indicators

In my Pound Value Forecasting project, I played a pivotal role in enhancing businesses' capabilities to make informed decisions in international markets. By analyzing multivariate time series data, including macro-economic indicators such as inflation rate, employment rate, CPI, and oil rate, I employed advanced models like Vector Autoregression (VAR), Support Vector Machines (SVM), and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) to forecast Pound foreign exchange values. Through a comparative study, LSTM emerged as the most accurate model, showcasing low Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE) in comparison to VAR and SVM. This project underscores the importance of accurate forecasting in empowering businesses to navigate currency risks, set competitive prices, and optimize supply chain management, ultimately contributing to better financial outcomes and enhanced credibility with stakeholders.
